Packaging

Wrapped with care

Every Tidal order is wrapped in tissue and nestled in our signature recycled kraft box. We use plastic-free packaging throughout — no polybags, no excess. Gift notes are always available at checkout.

Tracking

Always in the loop

You'll receive a confirmation email the moment your order is placed, and a dispatch email with your tracking link as soon as your parcel leaves us. You can track your order at any time from your account.

How to Return

Returns are free for UK orders. International returns are at the customer's cost.

01

Request

Email hello@tidalswimwear.com within 30 days of delivery with your order number and the items you'd like to return.

02

Receive Label

We'll send you a prepaid Royal Mail label (UK) or return instructions within 1 business day. No printing needed for QR code returns.

03

Post Back

Repack your items with the hygiene liner intact and drop them at your nearest Royal Mail collection point or Post Office.

04

Refund

Once received and inspected, your refund is processed within 3–5 business days to your original payment method.

Return Conditions

Items must be returned in their original, unworn condition with all tags attached and the hygiene liner in place. For hygiene reasons, we are unable to accept returns on swimwear where the hygiene liner has been removed. We reserve the right to decline returns that do not meet these conditions. Sale items are eligible for exchange or store credit only.

Exchanges

Prefer a different size?

We're happy to exchange for a different size or colour, subject to availability. Simply mention this when you email us to start your return and we'll hold the item while your return is in transit.

Refunds

Back to you swiftly

Refunds are issued to your original payment method within 3–5 business days of us receiving your return. You'll receive an email confirmation the moment we process it. Bank processing times may vary.
