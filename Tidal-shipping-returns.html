Tracking

Always in the loop

Returns

Please email hello@tidal-swimwear.co.uk for the returns address.

Orders placed before 1pm Monday to Friday are dispatched the same day. Orders placed after 1pm, or at the weekend, are dispatched the next working day. You'll receive a dispatch email with a tracking link as soon as your parcel leaves us.

We ship to over 40 countries worldwide, including all EU member states, the US, Canada, Australia, and New Zealand. If your country doesn't appear at checkout, please get in touch and we'll do our best to help.

For orders outside the UK, duties and import taxes may be applied by your country's customs authority. These charges are the responsibility of the recipient and are not included in our shipping fees. We recommend checking with your local customs office before ordering if you are unsure.

We process orders quickly, so there is only a short window to make changes. Please contact us immediately if you need to amend or cancel an order. If your order has already been dispatched, you will need to follow our returns process once it arrives.
