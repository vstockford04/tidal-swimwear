Size Guide

Every piece is cut for real bodies, made to move with you. Use these measurements as a starting point — our team is always on hand if you'd like personal guidance.

How to Measure

Use a soft measuring tape, held snug but not tight.

01

Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the floor. Breathe naturally — don't hold your breath.

02

Waist

Measure around your natural waist — the narrowest part of your torso, usually about an inch above your navel.

03

Hips

Stand with feet together and measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat, usually about 8 inches below your waist.

Fit Notes

How each style runs

The Soleil (Triangle Top)

Runs true to size. Adjustable ties give flexibility across a full size either way. Fuller busts may prefer sizing up.

The Côte (Bandeau)

Runs slightly small across the chest. We recommend sizing up if you are between sizes or prefer more coverage.

The Riviera (One-Piece)

Sized to bust. The adjustable straps and moderate cut make this a forgiving fit for most proportions.

The Neri (Tie-Side Bottom)

Sized to hips. The tie detail offers an adjustable waist — great if your waist and hips fall in different sizes.

Between sizes?

If your measurements fall between two sizes, we generally recommend sizing up for tops and one-pieces, and sizing down for bottoms — this tends to give the most comfortable and secure fit. All of our pieces are made with a small amount of stretch built in.
