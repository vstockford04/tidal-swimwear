Size Guide

Every piece is cut for real bodies, made to move with you. Use these measurements as a starting point — our team is always on hand if you'd like personal guidance.

How to Measure

Use a soft measuring tape, held snug but not tight.

01

Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape parallel to the floor. Breathe naturally — don't hold your breath.

02

Waist

Measure around your natural waist — the narrowest part of your torso, usually about an inch above your navel.

03

Hips

Stand with feet together and measure around the fullest part of your hips and seat, usually about 8 inches below your waist.
