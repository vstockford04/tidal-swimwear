Our Story

Where it all began

Tidal Swimwear began where so many of my favourite memories were made — at my family's house in St Tropez, summers spent in the sun with my two younger sisters, days that seemed to stretch on forever by the sea. Designing swimwear that captured the feeling of those summers felt like the most natural thing in the world. Each piece is sketched there, in the place that started it all, before travelling home to be brought to life in a small family-run atelier on the Dorset coast.

Photo coming soon — the house in St Tropez

We work only with the finest recycled Italian Lycra, transforming something discarded into something quietly luxurious. Every piece is cut, sewn, and finished by hand — considered, intentional, and never rushed. It's a slower way of making swimwear, but we wouldn't have it any other way.

Tidal is built on the belief that the best things in life are the ones made with love and made to last. We hope our pieces will become part of your own summers, your own memories, and the trips you'll one day tell your sisters, daughters, or dearest friends about.
